About

Jul 28th, 2013

Welcome to my blog! My name is Greg Boone, I work for Excella Consulting as a WordPress developer. I grew up in the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul, Minnesota. In previous lives I was a grad student at Georgetown University, an English teacher in Hungary, an English Teacher in South Korea, and a student at Gustavus Adolphus College.

I love technology, travel, biking, and home brewing. I write mostly about that first one here. I’m particularly interested in how technologies and society interact including issues like privacy, hacktivism, and education technology. There’s a good chance I’ll write about any of those things in addition to the things I learn in my day to day job developing WordPress. Interesting side note, even though I’m a WordPress developer, this blog runs Octopress. The rest of HarmsBoone.org runs WordPress, though.
